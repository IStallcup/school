Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Augute Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

  • Date: 1806
  • Medium: Oil on Canvas
  • Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
  • Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
